About Us
Coffee Shop is your go-to neighborhood joint, any time, any day.Join us for specialty coffe to pep you up in the morning, organic smoothies, juices, and food to fuel your day, and craft beer, wine, and cocktails to get your night started!
Our Services

Long Black

Long Black is hot water with an espresso shot extracted on top of the hot water.

Espresso

Espresso is the foundation and the most important part to every espresso based drink.

Double Espresso

Double Espresso is just that, two espresso shots in one cup.

Cafe Latte

Café Latte is an espresso based drink with steamed milk and micro-foam added to the coffee
